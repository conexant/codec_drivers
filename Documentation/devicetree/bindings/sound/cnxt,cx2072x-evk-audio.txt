Conexant System - cx2072x audio codec.

Required properties:

- compatible - "string" - One of:
    "cnxt,cx2072x-evk-audio" - cx2072x audio codec device


Optional properties:

- ti,hp-det-gpio - gpio pin number used for jack detection notification
- ti,codec-clock-rate - system clock used for codec's MCLK rate

Example:

sound {
	compatible = "cnxt,cx2072x-evm-audio";
	ti,codec-clock-rate = <24000000>;
	ti,hp-det-gpio = <&gpio3 19 0x1>;
};
